What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of data stored on a user's device by a website. These cookies help websites remember user preferences, track user behavior, and improve user experience.To find out more about Cookies, including how to see what Cookies have been set on your computer and how to manage and delete them, visit www.allaboutcookies.org. At Website Soirees Designs, we understand the importance of transparency and compliance when it comes to cookies policies. Our cookies policy at Website Soirees Designs outlines the types of Cookies used on our website, including essential, functional, and performance cookies. Essential cookies are necessary for the website to function properly, while functional cookies help improve user experience by remembering user preferences. Performance cookies track user behavior on the website to help us analyze and improve our website's performance. We also use third-party cookies on our website to help us track user behavior and improve our marketing efforts. These cookies are used to analyze user demographics, interests, and behavior to help us create targeted advertising campaigns. Users can easily disable cookies through their browser settings or use opt-out tools provided by third-party vendors.
