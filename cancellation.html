Soirée's Designs Cancellation Policy

HOME / Cancellation Policy

Customers have up to 24 hours after placing their order to cancel it without incurring any fees. This gives customers a reasonable amount of time to change their minds or make any necessary adjustments to their order. If a customer wishes to cancel their order after the 24-hour window has passed, they may still do so, but they will be subject to a cancellation fee. The fee is typically a percentage of the total order amount and is intended to cover any costs incurred by the business in processing the order. This is a standard practice in the retail industry and helps to ensure that businesses are not left with unsellable inventory or wasted resources. Customers who cancel their orders are typically refunded the full amount of their order, minus the cancellation fee. This refund is typically processed within a few business days and is returned to the original form of payment used to make the purchase. This ensures that customers are not only able to cancel their orders but also receive a timely refund for any payments made. Custom-made or personalized items may not be eligible for cancellation once production has begun. This is because these items are made specifically for the customer and cannot be resold. Additionally, clearance or sale items may also be ineligible for cancellation, as these items are typically sold at a discount and may have limited availability. Customers who wish to cancel their order must do so by contacting Soirees Designs directly. This can usually be done via email, at soiressdesigns@gmail.com or through the company's website. It is important for customers to provide their order number and any other relevant information when requesting a cancellation to ensure that their request is processed promptly.
